## OVERVIEW

Forgotten Fruit is an experimental commerce framework linking agricultural production with tokenized ownership and exchange. It operates across multiple implementations, including Peach Tycoon and a reclaimed grape-to-wine pipeline. The system enables digital assets to function as claims on real-world goods.

## SYSTEM COMPONENTS

**Tokenized Asset Layer**
NFTs representing claimable fruit or wine units.

**Digi-Physical Redemption System**
Mechanism for converting tokens into physical goods.

**Agricultural Supply Integration**
Sourcing produce — peaches, unused grapes — from real-world producers.

**Market Interface**
Enables purchase, transfer, gifting, and resale of tokenized goods.

**Seasonal Production Cycles**
Time-bound releases tied to harvest and production windows.

## INTERPRETATION

Forgotten Fruit treats physical goods as programmable assets. Ownership, transfer, and redemption are abstracted into a unified token layer. This collapses the boundary between digital markets and agricultural supply chains.

The system explores how perishability, seasonality, and surplus can be encoded into commerce primitives. It suggests a model where production cycles and market dynamics are directly coupled.
